### Clinical Mechanism
The interaction between DPYD and warfarin has significant clinical implications. DPYD is primarily responsible for the metabolism of warfarin. 

### Clinical Consequence
Poor metabolizers of DPYD experience significantly altered systemic exposure to warfarin, leading to increased risk of toxicity or lack of efficacy. Normal metabolizers achieve standard therapeutic concentrations.

### CPIC Dosing Recommendation
For poor metabolizers, consider an alternative medication or a drastic dose reduction (e.g., 50%). For rapid/ultrarapid metabolizers, standard dosing may be inadequate, requiring close monitoring or alternative therapy.
